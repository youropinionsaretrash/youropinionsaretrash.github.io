About the project


”Your Opinions Are Trash” was created in ES 125H The Climate Crisis and The Liberal Arts during the Spring 2023 Semester. The project was created with the intention of promoting awareness about the climate crisis through a public, interactive installation. Inspired by similar campaigns that occurred in Boston in 2016, “Your Opinions Are Trash” encourages disposal of small litter or waste into a receptacle that looks similar to a ballot box. People can vote in response to the provided prompt by tossing their trash into the corresponding bin. By using the ballot box, people reduce waste that may normally end up in the street, while learning facts about the climate crisis. Through a scannable QR code, people can learn more information about climate change, as well as find local resources and calls for action on how they can help change the climate crisis.
